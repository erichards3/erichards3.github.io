About me

My name is Edwin, but you can call me Eddie. I currently work as a full-stack software developer for an automation software company based in Southern Arizona where I have lived the majority of my life. I discovered my passion for software development while taking courses at a local community college and stumbling upon the Computer Programming program they offered. I distinctly remember starting my first course in the program ("Intro to Logic") and I almost couldn't put down the textbook! Everything just made so much sense! At that point, I knew I wanted write software professionally and I have been living that dream since 2017!

My areas of expertise lie primarly in Windows-based software development but I am always eager to learn new languages and technologies. I use C#.Net (and .Net Core) and MS SQL Server at work and I especially enjoy working in the back end, though I can get by doing front end development as well (I did make this website, after all). I have a working knowledge of several programming languages including C#, C/C++, Java, and Python and I am very well versed in Object Oriented Programming concepts and principles.

As far as education goes, I am always learning new things, but I currently possess an Associate's degree in computer programming from the community college I mentioned earlier as well as a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I am also considering pursuing a Master's degree in CS, but I have not committed to anything just yet.

In addition to my passion for writing software, I also have a passion for teaching software development concepts to people of all ages! I have spent the past year or so tutoring CS and IT students who are brand new to coding. I love having the opportunity to share my knowledge and experience with them and getting them to that "Ah-ha!" moment!

When I am not working, studying, or teaching, I like to spend time playing with my kids, watching movies and TV shows, playing watching football, and working on personal coding projects.
